Hi there! Thank you for visiting my page. I am a proud Arizona State University Sun Devil pursuing a bachelor of science degree in Chemistry in the School of Molecular Sciences, a minor degree in Sustainability in the School of Sustainability, and a minor degree in Data Science in the School of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences. I am completing my undergraduate Honors thesis funded by NSF in association with the Central Arizona Phoenix Long Term Ecological Research team. I am investigating the potential for a constructed wastewater treatment wetland in filtering trace organic contaminants of emerging concern such as plastic additives and per/poly-fluoroalkyl substances. I will be presenting my findings at the annual CAP LTER research symposium in 2023. I will be attending graduate school to earn my PhD in Chemistry once I graduate in May 2023. I am actively exploring new opportunities in environmental chemistry and would love to hear about any opportunities that I would be a good fit for. I am a dedicated and self-disciplined researcher that will bring an ambitious and friendly attitude to any position I attain. Feel free to reach out and tell me about what you are currently working on!
